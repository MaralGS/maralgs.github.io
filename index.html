About me

Hi! My name is Alex Garcia Selma, and I am a passionate video game developer with a degree in Video Game Development and Design from CITM-UPC. Ever since I can remember, video games have been my great passion, both playing them and creating them.
I've been fortunate enough to turn my love for video games into a career. Throughout my education and career, I have acquired skills in various areas, programming, engine programming, design, level design,etc.
